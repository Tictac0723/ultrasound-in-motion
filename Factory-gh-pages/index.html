Services

3D/4D Fetal Ultrasound

We are qualified to do both elective ultrasounds and diagnostic ultrasounds. All studies will be performed by Registered Diagnostic Medical Sonographers and Ultrasound in Motion adheres to strict guidelines in accordance with the FDA to ensure a safe environment for your prenatal and vascular screening exams.

Mobile Services

We can bring our ultrasound machine to physicians offices and clinics and perform diagnostic ultrasounds. Please call our office for more information on mobile services.

Competitive Pricing

We offer competitive pricing, a convenient location, weekday and Saturday appointments and the most comprehensive 3D/4D and Vascular Screening packages.
